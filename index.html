Tower of Hanoi

1) Only 1 disc can be moved at a time
2) No disc may be placed on top of a smaller disc

Project: Eduto

A one-stop platform to enhance the students’ learning process and automate school administrative tasks. For instance, with bluetooth beacons strategically placed in school, attendance will be taken automatically through the students' phones. For the positive impact this made on the community, the project received Silver in the SITF Awards, an industry-championed accolade for made-in Singapore infocomm innovations

Project: Socialshield

A web plugin to protect young children and PTSD patients from potential triggers and sensitive material online. The plugin leverages on natural language processing & machine learning to accurately analyze content. in 2016, the project was nominated from 100s of projects to represent Singapore in Taiwan for the Asia Pacific ICT Alliance (APICTA) Awards.
